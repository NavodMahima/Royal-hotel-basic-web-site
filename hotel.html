Come & Rest

We provide the facilities to our guests to travel into the Sinharaja rain Forest, one of the biodiversity hotspots in the country and to observe the beautiful waterfalls, endemic fauna & flora. Eco Villa is the best location you can have in Deniyaya with staggering views of a rain forest with delicious homemade food.

Home Made Food

We use organic rice, fresh vegetables and fruits grown in our premises to prepare food, so that the guests can experience the taste of the Sri Lankan cuisine. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served with a vast variety of traditional foods so that the travellers can select the food according to their taste.

Unforgettable Experiences

The guests also can involve in hiking,trekking, swimming in waterfalls, bird watching and fish therapy with an experienced person, Bandula Rathnayaka. We provide calm and quite environment to the guests with numerous facilities and warm hospitality to spend thier tour.
