Our Services

Fitness and wellness

Recharge your mind and body with our fitness and wellness services. Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced trainers, and rejuvenating spa treatments will help you achieve your fitness goals and leave you feeling refreshed and revitalized. Elevate your stay with our wellness services.

Entertainment

Experience the perfect blend of relaxation and entertainment during your stay with us. From live music performances to exciting game rooms, we offer a range of entertainment options to keep you engaged and energized throughout your stay. Unwind, have fun, and create unforgettable memories with your loved ones at our hotel

Spa Services

Enjoy a clean and comfortable stay with our daily housekeeping service. Our friendly and professional staff will ensure that your room is always fresh and tidy, allowing you to relax and unwind.

Swimming Pool

Take a dip and escape the heat in our luxurious swimming pool. Surrounded by beautiful landscaping and equipped with comfortable loungers, our pool area is the perfect spot to relax and rejuvenate. Whether you're a serious swimmer or just looking to cool off, our pool is the ideal retreat.

Beach Access

Escape to a world of sun, sand, and sea with our beach access service. Enjoy a refreshing swim, bask in the sun, or take a relaxing walk on the shore. Our beachfront location provides the perfect setting for a memorable seaside vacation. Come and experience the beauty of the beach with us.

Library

Indulge in a literary adventure with our library service. Immerse yourself in the world of books, relax in a cozy corner, and let your imagination soar. With our extensive collection of books and comfortable reading spaces, we offer the perfect retreat for book lovers. Come and discover new worlds with us.

Movie Nights

Experience the magic of cinema under the stars with our movie nights service. Sit back, relax, and enjoy a movie with your loved ones in a cozy and intimate setting. With our state-of-the-art equipment and diverse movie selection, you're sure to have a memorable evening with us.

Car Renatal

Explore the local area with ease by renting a car through our hotel. Our car rental service offers convenient and affordable options to help you make the most of your stay.

Free Wi-Fi

Get connected with our complimentary high-speed WiFi during your stay. Our fast and reliable WiFi is available in your room and common areas. Stay connected while enjoying your visit.

Laundry

Take the hassle out of laundry during your stay with our convenient laundry service. Our professional and efficient service will ensure that your clothes are clean and fresh, ready for your next adventure.

Foods & Beverages

Indulge in delicious cuisine and refreshing beverages at our hotel. From gourmet dining to casual snacks and cocktails, we offer a range of options to satisfy any palate.

Airport Shuttle(Surcharge)

Travel to and from the airport with ease through our convenient shuttle service (surcharge). Our professional and reliable service will ensure a stress-free start or end to your journey.

Daily House Keeping

Enjoy a clean and comfortable stay with our daily housekeeping service. Our friendly and professional staff will ensure that your room is always fresh and tidy, allowing you to relax and unwind.

Wine Tasting

Embark on a sensory journey with our wine tasting service. Sample the finest wines from around the world, learn about their history and production, and savor the unique flavors and aromas. With our expert sommeliers and carefully curated wine selection, we offer a truly unforgettable wine tasting experience. Join us and raise a glass to the good life.

Yoga Classes

Find inner peace and balance with our yoga classes service. Unwind, stretch, and strengthen your body and mind in a serene and tranquil setting. With our experienced instructors and diverse yoga styles, we cater to all levels and preferences. Join us and take the first step towards a healthier and happier you.
